Lasagna Recipe

Description

This page will show you how to make delicious home-cooked fried ice cream.

Ingredients

  • Instant fried ice cream In a Box
  • Garfield
  • Some whip cream

Steps

  1. first make some preparation. Wash your hand and preheat the pan with oil.
  2. second, put the Instant fried ice cream into the pan. heat for 1 minutes.
  3. put extra whip cream if you want. Garfield love whip cream
  4. enjoy!
