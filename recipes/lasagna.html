Lasagna Recipe

Description

This page will show you how to make delicious home-cooked lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Instant Lasagna In a Box
  • Garfield
  • Some extra cheese

Steps

  1. first make some preparation. Wash your hand and preheat the oven.
  2. second, put the Instant Lasagna into the microwave. heat for 20 minutes.
  3. put extra cheese if you want. Garfield love cheese
  4. enjoy!
